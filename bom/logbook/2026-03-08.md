## Interpretation

FP16/BF16 throughput reaches approximately 120+ TFLOPS.
This corresponds to roughly 60–65% of theoretical peak matrix throughput.

FP32 throughput improves significantly after enabling PyTorch TunableOp autotuning.

---

## Conclusion

The ROCm 7.2 stack and PyTorch ROCm backend operate correctly on Radeon AI PRO R9700.

GPU compute pipeline validated:

PyTorch → HIP → rocBLAS → GPU

System is ready for AI workloads including:

- LLM inference
- mixed precision training
- diffusion models
