## Sanity Check

```bash
for dir in ~/models/*/; do
    name=$(basename "$dir")
    size=$(du -sh "$dir" 2>/dev/null | cut -f1)
    safetensors=$(find "$dir" -maxdepth 1 -name "*.safetensors" -type f | wc -l)
    incomplete=$(find "$dir" -name "*.incomplete" -type f 2>/dev/null | wc -l)
    echo "$name: $size | safetensors=$safetensors | incomplete=$incomplete"
done
```

All 11 models: `incomplete=0`, shard counts match `model.safetensors.index.json`.
